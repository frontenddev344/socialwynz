Personal Information Policy

At Social Wynz, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring your privacy. This Personal Information Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you access our website and services.

Information We Collect

When you interact with Social Wynz, we may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
  • Game Activity: Information about your gameplay, including scores, achievements, and preferences.
  • Usage Data: Details about how you use our website, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services and games.
  • To communicate with you, including sending updates, promotions, and newsletters.
  • To analyze usage and trends to enhance your experience.
  • To ensure compliance with our terms of service and applicable laws.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may engage third-party companies to assist us in operating our website and services. These service providers are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes for which it was shared.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your personal information if required by law, or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@socialwynz.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Personal Information Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website with a revised effective date. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Personal Information Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

info@socialwynz.com
